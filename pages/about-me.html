Michal Parusinski - Personal Site

Michal Parusinski is a software developer, mathematician and technical leader. After finishing his studies at Imperial College London, he did research work at Université Catholique de Louvain's Crypto group, worked at IBM on Business Analytics software and as technical leader at Amadeus in Sophia Antipolis. He is currently working as a research engineer for SERTIT ICube which is part of the University of Strasbourg. He also enjoys playing go, practising martial arts and studying languages.
